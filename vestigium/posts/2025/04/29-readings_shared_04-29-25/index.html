  • [[https://scindeks-clanci.ceon.rs/data/pdf/3042-2469/2024/3042-24692402037S.pdf][Improving mathematical proving skills through interactive theorem proving]]. ~ Sana Stojanović Đurđević, Andrija Urošević, Filip Marić (2024). #ITP #IsabelleHOL #Math The article explores how interactive theorem provers, such as Isabelle, can enhance students’ mathematical proof skills. Through an elective course at the University of Belgrade, students used these tools to construct formal proofs and verify algorithms, receiving immediate feedback that reinforced logical reasoning. The results showed significant improvement in students' ability to write precise and correct proofs, suggesting that integrating such technology into math education can effectively support the development of rigorous mathematical thinking.
  • [[https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.19270][Formalising the local compactness of the adele ring]]. ~ Salvatore Mercuri. #ITP #LeanProver #Math This article presents a complete formalization, using the Lean 4 proof assistant, of the fundamental property of local compactness of the adele ring of a number field. The adele ring is a central construction in modern number theory whose local compactness is crucial for applying analytical techniques to arithmetic problems. The work includes the formalization of related concepts, such as the different completions of a number field and the rings of integers at the finite completions, formally proving their local compactness or compactness as appropriate, culminating in a computer-verified proof of the local compactness of the full adele ring.
